Meet the Cultural Architect

Shane Tanguis

For as long as I can remember, I've had a fascination with the circumstances that made me into who I am today.

How do I treat others? What do I do with my time? What must I do to be loved and accepted? Apart from genetics and chance, I found it was the ideas prioritized in my surroundings that shaped these and the thousands of decisions that have determined my destiny.

These ideas became the Operating System of my Mind, forming a decision-making structure not unlike a set of algorithms: In this situation, zig. In that one, zag. Whereas a true OS can be downloaded in minutes, the OS of my mind was written over many years through countless repetition of ideas and by my unique emotionally-relevant experiences, only some of which I understood in the fog and wonder of youth.

A wealthy man once told me a story. One of his earliest memories was of his father ignoring a homeless person. After decades of failed relationships and estrangement from loved ones, he felt this single experience had helped forge in his mind the regretful idea that compassion and human connection were for the weak. This chance misperception of his five-year-old self changed his life forever.

This is why I created the Wedding Legacy and the Cultureware App.

The ideal Wedding Legacy associates powerful imagery and inspirational ideas with a last name, tapping into every human being's deeply-seated desires to both belong to something greater than ourselves and to gain our family's love and acceptance.

Each Legacy is deeply personal to me and isn't complete until you are proud to display it in your home.
