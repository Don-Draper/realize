Click on the image or the text. Once clicked, you can drag to move or change size by dragging a corner square. Buttons will appear on the left when images or text are selected. Change the background color with the top left button. Upload an image of your own with the button to its right. Add any amount of images on the left by clicking on them. When you're done, click the Download button and you'll get a PNG image to keep.
